carrot cake

description

Here's an old-fashioned carrot cake that's got more carrots than spice. Pecans can be substituted for walnuts

ingredients

  • grated carrots
  • pecans
  • flour
  • eggs
  • butter
  • sugar

steps

  1. preheat oven and grease pan
  2. mix all ingredients
  3. bake for 55 minutes
